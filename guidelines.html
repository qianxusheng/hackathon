Community Guidelines

Welcome to Resonate Frequency

Our community is dedicated to fostering a positive and inclusive environment. Please follow these guidelines to help us maintain a friendly and respectful space for everyone.

General Guidelines

  • Be respectful and considerate in your interactions with others.
  • Avoid personal attacks, harassment, and hate speech.
  • Encourage constructive discussions and provide helpful feedback.
  • Report any instances of online hate or inappropriate behavior to the moderators.
  • Respect the privacy of others and do not share personal information without consent.

Posting Guidelines

  • Ensure your posts are relevant to the community and contribute to the discussion.
  • Avoid spamming or posting repetitive content.
  • Use appropriate language and avoid offensive or inflammatory remarks.
  • Provide sources and references for any claims or information shared.
  • Encourage positive contributions by liking and promoting excellent discussions.

Reporting Guidelines

If you encounter any form of online hate, harassment, or inappropriate behavior, please report it immediately. Your reports help us keep the community safe and welcoming for everyone.

Encouraging Positive Contributions

Show your appreciation for valuable contributions by liking excellent discussions. This helps highlight positive content and encourages others to contribute meaningfully.
