Maintaining a Friendly Community

At Resonate Frequency, we believe in fostering a positive and inclusive online environment. Here are some tips to help maintain a friendly community:

  • Be respectful and considerate in your interactions with others.
  • Encourage constructive discussions and avoid personal attacks.
  • Report any instances of online hate or harassment to the moderators.
  • Like and promote excellent discussions to encourage positive contributions.

Encouraging Reporting of Online Hate

If you encounter any form of online hate or harassment, please report it immediately. Your reports help us keep the community safe and welcoming for everyone.

Encouraging Positive Contributions

Show your appreciation for valuable contributions by liking excellent discussions. This helps highlight positive content and encourages others to contribute meaningfully.
